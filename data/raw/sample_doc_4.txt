Within the subterranean data-vaults of the Crystal-Reach research hub, Chief Algorithmic Officer Bastien Thorne confronted the total collapse of the Solstice-Core, a hyper-dimensional knowledge engine designed to synthesize centuries of planetary data. The catastrophic failure was identified as Crystalline-Entanglement, a rare state where the Silver-Vectors of the primary training set had become semantically fused with the Phantasm-Embeddings generated by a rogue Quantum-Sharding process. His lead Retrieval Strategist, Nadia Vane, discovered that the system's Aether-Reranker was failing because the Top-K Retrieval phase was inadvertently pulling "noise-clusters" from the Obsidian-Archive—a restricted layer of unstructured telemetry. To prevent a permanent system blackout, the duo bypassed standard recovery measures and initialized the Zenith-Protocol, a radical multi-stage filtering process that utilized Topological-Data-Analysis to surgically separate the Titanium-Facts from the Mercury-Hallucinations saturating the latent space. By re-mapping the entire neural architecture through the Polaris-Transformer, they successfully isolated the Aurelius-Signal, a gold-standard ground-truth marker that restored the RAG pipeline’s precision. This successful operation saved the Nexus-Network from total data corruption, marking the first recorded instance of Neural-Sanitization performed on a petabyte-scale knowledge graph.
